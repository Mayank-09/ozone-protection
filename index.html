Why we need ozone?






Ozone layer saves us from the UV rays produced by the sun.
If we deplete it the Ultraviolet rays produced from the sun leads us to many diseases and allergies. So we should save ozone






How we can Protect our Ozone Layer !!



  1. Avoid the consumption of gases dangerous to the ozone layer, due to their content or manufacturing process.
  2. Minimize the use of cars.
  3. Do not use cleaning products that are harmful to the environment and to us.
  4. Buy local products.
  5. For travelling short distances use cycles or walk.
  6. Purify smoke from factories before releasing them.
  7. Use vehicles with CNG Engine.
